The following are specialist Wizard spell package files that are
assigned by packages.2da. They are used in cases of auto-leveling
with the LevelUpHenchman() call. They can also be called by the
'Set to Package Default' button under the character blueprint
Spells tab.

In each case, the list contains some spells from the opposition
school and is missing a few spells from the specialist school.
Where possible, I replaced selected opposition spells with the
missing specialist spells, or else with a non-opposition spell.
I moved the modified specialist spell rows up the spell level
list so the character is more likely to gain the spell on level up.
